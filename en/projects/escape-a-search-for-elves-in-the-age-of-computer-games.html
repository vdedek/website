▓ Escape: A Search for Elves in the Age of Computer Games
Type: Text, Research Year: 2022 Published in: Klima n° 05 – Liquid Futures Publisher: Les presses du réel

About the publication
Published in Klima n° 05 – Liquid Futures, an issue approaching the aquatic universe, exploring the space of permanent composting, the various living and non-living entities in seas and oceans, as well as the myths and fantasies stemming from them.
The text comes from my audio work The Last Advanced Movement Guide You Will Need and flowcharts are based on my artistic research project The Slippery Slope of Gaming Experience, examining the relationship between gaming, mythology, and escapism.

Year: 2022
Context: Liste Art Fair Basel, Switzerland
